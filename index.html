03 — Field notes
Behavior

The farmer optimizes for the minimum viable action.

If a quest can be scripted, it already is. The lowest-cost repeatable unit wins every leaderboard.

Exploit

Opaque points systems leak faster than token drops.

Hidden formulas get reverse-engineered in days. Asymmetric information favors whoever is watching most closely.

Retention

Retention fails because the product was never the reward.

When the incentive ends, the behavior ends. Real users come back for reasons that exist outside the drop.

Detection

Wallet clustering alone catches only the lazy ones.

Funding graphs, timing, and behavioral entropy matter more than the obvious tells. The good farms figured this out years ago.
